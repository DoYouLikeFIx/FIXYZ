## Story

As an execution engine owner,  
I want a canonical matching engine core for LIMIT and MARKET rules,  
So that execution behavior is deterministic and policy-compliant.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given LIMIT order and opposite-book liquidity When price-cross condition is met Then fills are generated by strict price-time priority and leaves quantity is updated correctly.
2. Given LIMIT order with no price cross When matching executes Then no fill is created and order remains `NEW` with full leaves quantity.
3. Given MARKET order When opposite-book liquidity exists Then engine sweeps levels in strict price-time order until requested quantity is exhausted.
4. Given MARKET order with partial opposite-book liquidity When matching executes Then partial fills are produced and final state is `PARTIALLY_FILLED` with correct `leavesQty`.
5. Given MARKET order with empty opposite-book liquidity When matching executes Then deterministic reject contract is returned as `HTTP 422 + ORD-013 (NO_OPPOSITE_LIQUIDITY)` and no fill artifacts are emitted.
6. Given matching output is produced When downstream persistence layer consumes result Then result payload is deterministic (`fills[]`, `totalExecutedQty`, `leavesQty`, `weightedAvgPrice`, `rejectCode`) and free of side effects.
